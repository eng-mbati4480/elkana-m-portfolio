## Setup Instructions

### 1. **Add Your Photo**
Replace `your-photo.jpg` with your actual profile photo
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels or larger (square aspect ratio works best)
- The image will be automatically cropped to a circle

### 2. **Add Background Video**
Replace `water-engineering-video.mp4` with your water engineering video:
- **Recommended Sources for Videos:**
  - Pexels.com (free stock videos)
  - Pixabay.com (water, engineering, irrigation videos)
  - Unsplash.com (nature/water footage)
  - YouTube (download water engineering documentaries)
- **Video Specifications:**
  - Format: MP4 (primary) and WebM (optional for better browser support)
  - Duration: 10-30 seconds (videos loop automatically)
  - Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
  - Content: Water flow, irrigation systems, engineering work, nature scenes
- **File Size:** Keep under 10MB for fast loading

### 3. **Customize Contact Information**
Update the contact details in `elkana.html`:
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location

### 4. **Open the Website**
Double-click `elkana.html` or open it in any modern web browser

## Customization

To customize this portfolio:

1. **Personal Information**: Update the content in `elkana.html`
2. **Styling**: Modify colors, fonts, and layout in `elkana.css`
3. **Functionality**: Add new features or modify existing ones in `elkana.js`
4. **Contact Information**: Update email, phone, and location details
